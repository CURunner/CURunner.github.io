CU Buffs Fueling Station App Privacy Policy

Last updated: 03-04-2022

Data we collect

When you check in, data you filled out in your profile is uploaded to a database that is only accessible by the performance nutrition staff. Data we collect when you check in:
  • Your Name
  • Your Sport
  • Your Identikey
  • The time you checked in
  • Any pickup names you provide

Other data included

  • App version number
  • If smart check in was used
  • A random userID (random string of numbers and letters)
  • Usage data such as how popular certain app screens are.
  • Ralphie Run High Scores

Crash Data

If you have opted into sending crash reports on your device, if the app ever crashes, some crash data will be sent to Jake to help fix app bugs. This data is completely confidential and has no personal information attached.

Automatic check in

The fueling station app supports automatic check in to check you in without opening the app. For this feature to work you must enable always allow for location services. Even though always on location is required to enabled, no GPS data is used. Instead, your device listens for the presence of a Bluetooth beacon located in the fueling station. When the presence of the beacon is detected, you are check in. Your location information is not stored or accessed in any way.

Gold card integration

The fueling station app lets you link your Gold Card account (provided by the Flatiron Meal Plan) with the app for easy balance checking. When you link your account your Gold Card username and password are saved locally to your device. Your Gold Card password is encrypted with AES encryption when it is stored on your device to protect it at rest. When you refresh your balance, your device logs into your Gold Card account on the Flatiron Meal Plan website. Because the app interacts with the Flatiron Meal Plan website, their privacy policy is also worth reading.

Nutrition Notes

Your nutrition notes are stored in on your device and are not accessible by anyone other than yourself.

This privacy policy is subject to change. By using the CU Buffs Fueling Station app you agree to this policy. If you do not want to use this app to check in, the staff offer a kiosk system for you to check in manually.

If you have any questions please do not hesitate to reach out to me.
